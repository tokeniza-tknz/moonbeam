# TKNZ Tokenomics

The TKNZ token is the central utility token of the Tokeniza ecosystem. The entire economic model is designed to foster community growth, fund platform development, and reward participation.

## Total Supply and Allocation

**Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 TKNZ (1 Billion)

The total supply is allocated into four primary categories:

| Category | Token Amount | Percentage | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Community** | 500,000,000 | 50% | TGE, community incentives, airdrops, and ecosystem rewards. |
| **Foundation** | 330,000,000 | 33% | Treasury, new product development, and potential liquidity. |
| **Contributors** | 100,000,000 | 10% | Vesting for team, advisors, and strategic partners. |
| **Private Sale** | 70,000,000 | 7% | Raise capital for platform expansion and new features. |
